The Problem

Losing items on campus
is stressful & chaotic

🔍

No Central System

Lost items are reported through scattered Facebook groups, bulletin boards, and word-of-mouth — nothing unified.

⏳

Time-Sensitive

The longer an item stays unclaimed, the harder it is to recover. Speed matters — but the current process is slow.

🔒

Privacy Concerns

Posting personal items publicly exposes sensitive info. Students need a way to report without compromising privacy.

02 / 07
The Solution

One platform.
Every lost item.

UST Lost & Found is a dedicated web app where students can report, browse, and claim lost or found items across all campus locations.

📜

Easy Reporting

Submit lost or found items in seconds with category, location, date, and optional photo uploads.

📍

Campus Locations

14 pre-defined campus zones — Main Building, Benavides, Plaza Mayor, Arch of the Centuries, and more.

🕵

Privacy Protection

Sensitive items are automatically anonymized. Contact details are hidden until ownership is verified.

📊

Live Dashboard

Real-time stats on open cases, recent reports, and resolved reunions — all at a glance.

03 / 07
How It Works

Three simple steps

1

Report

Describe the item, select the campus location, choose a category, and submit your report.

2

Browse & Match

Search through reported items by type, category, or location. Find what you're looking for fast.

3

Claim & Reunite

Found a match? Claim the item, verify ownership, and get reunited with your belongings.
